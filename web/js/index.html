  • Whom can we trust today?

    What’s wrong with the world these days? So much fake, make up, stalking, fishing, spam, hidden traps and unwanted add ups to the bills. To make collaborative consumption happen, we have to rely on: „oh, don’t worry. I bring your car back tomorrow!“ On Polly & Bob we want to establish a culture of trust. We count contribution to the community in community points and we exclude trolls and abusers (the few that disturb the system).

    Once we trust each other we can let go easier.

  • Sharing on Polly & Bob.

    Use what others have. Share what you have. „Who lends me his car for small money?“ „Who needs a babysitter tonight?“ „Who finishes the food I can’t eat before holydays?“ Swap, sell, share, give, hire, rent, take, use stuff around you. Hire or offer talents and services in the neighborhood.

    Polly & Bob brings good people together.

  • Welcome to the New Neighborhood.

    The new neighborhood we want to form is bringing people together in real life. The new neighborhood is open minded, sharing and connected by individual initiatives. It is slow. The new neighborhood is changing the world without going on the barricades. The new neighborhood is a place where trust can grow. This will take same time and affords. Let us start it today.

    A new neighborhood is a place where I can belong to.

  • Polly & Bob is a shared platform.

    Take part and take your part. The users create the value of the platform. The profits shall be shared between us also. Budgets shall be redirected to our neighborhoods, the place where profits appear. Everybody can buy his share of the company and take part in the decision making. Polly & Bob is the first social network which is owned by its users.

    Buy your share of 50 € and contact volker@pollyandbob.com now!

  • Small data is not watching you.

    Big Data is centralized data. Centralized information that can be abused to control and spam everybody with centralized information, and advertisement. Polly & Bob aims to be small data as much as possible. Data should be on local servers. You own your data. We don‘t encourage to post private pictures which are meant only to friends. Small data is stored locally not accessible centrally.

    Our data belongs to us.

  • Create your own community.

    Polly & Bob is a new way of living together with the people around you. It is not the muggy and airless neighborhood we know. The New Neighborhood community doesn’t have bureaucracy and fixed structures. It is fresh air and individualized. Everybody can start something, everybody can join. Everybody creates his own crowd and community around him. Polly & Bob communities are not bound to political districts. Individual communities overlap and form a new connectiveness in the local area. It is individual but not individualized. Nobody stays separate.

    Start by adding neighbors to you MyNeighbors list.

  • What is the human being about?

    Eyes can only meet in real life. On Polly & Bob we do clickclickclick and touch-screentouch. But we do it in order to get together offline. We say: ‘Take the time. Enrich your life', because we believe the human condition is about to meet and spend time with other people. We know it is easier to sit on a couch and watch. But we know also that it is much more rewarding to meet in Real life.

    Real life is what life really is about.

  • What‘s up around me?

    Where is something going on now? Who is free for having a coffee? Who joins in for the football match tonight? Who wants to explore the city together? Who organizes exciting stuff on the weekend? Who is having dinner with me today?

    Fun, around me, any time. Find it on Polly & Bob.

  • So much stuff unused.

    Millions of cars are standing in the streets. So much stuff collects dust in the junk room, self storages and even living rooms of our world. Why not use it? Why do we have to own it? Everything we need is access. The 21st century will see a dramatic change in the way we consume. The economy will turn from a possession economy to an access economy. We can organize this on Polly & Bob.

    Own less, have more. It’s access stupid!
